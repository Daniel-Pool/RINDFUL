## ‚ù§Ô∏è Solution
### RINDFUL allows users to daily journal and log their moods (using a citrus-inspired scale) in a safe environment. Users can view their mood progress by seeing graphical representations of how their mood changed over different time frames (hours, days, weeks, etc‚Ä¶). 
Plainly put: the goal of this app is to offer a lifeline to a prospective user, a space where they can focus their attention inward instead of outward, via earnest expression and reflection.
